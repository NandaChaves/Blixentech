The Game Awards 2021 - December 9th, 2021

Geoff Keighley's confirmed that The Game Awards are coming back on December 9th, hosting an in-person event at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles. Nominees will be revealed on Tuesday 16th November and there'll be new game announcements "in the double digits." That'll be streamed worldwide at 5pm PST / 8am EST, expected to run for 3 hours. As for the UK and Europe, that goes into the early hours of December 10th, going live at 1am GMT / 2am CET.

HP's new cases have a separate 'Cryo Chamber' for cooling

PC cases are having an interesting showing at this years’ CES with offerings like this one with automatically opening and closing vents. HP recently debuted its new Omen 27u monitor, and also showed off a new PC gaming case with a different, less cool looking but also likely much cheaper, innovative way of keeping cool. The new Omen 45L ATX case features a patented Omen Cryo Chamber that sits on top of the main case. It can support up AiO liquid coolers up to 360mm in size, and works by pulling cooler air from outside the tower into this chamber. It makes sense that this would be cooler than using air from already inside the case, and there’s a similar intake on the front too.

O mercado de jogos após a pandemia

Desde o início da pandemia, a procura pelo lazer e sociabilização proporcionados pelos videogames por quem está em casa alavancou ainda mais essa indústria criativa. Segundo o Global Games Market Report, da Newzoo, o mercado de jogos deve movimentar cerca de $189.3 bilhões de dólares em 2021.Hoje, são frequentes os editais, festivais e concursos de jogos pelo país. A PUCPR está inserida nesse circuito, pois promove anualmente a Global Game Jam, que é um dos maiores e mais produtivos hackatons dessa iniciativa no mundo.
